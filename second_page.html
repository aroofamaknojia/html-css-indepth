Our Story

The Beginnings of Something Revolutionary

AssignHub was founded in 2020 by a group of four USC students, Aroofa Maknojia, Jonathan Liu, Steven Gong, and Alishaa Khoslaa. We all started off as new recruits together in Business Tech Group (BTG) where we were tasked to build new technologies that would solve problems brought by the pandemic in various industries.

From the very beginning, we've always been driven to create a lasting solution that would help our peers stay organized and improve their mental health. The calendar options available for students by USC through Blackboard had been insufficient for the heavy workload that students had in an online environment. As students ourselves, we were frustrated by the time-consuming process of manually inputting all of our assignments for each of our classes based on our syllabi. Oftentimes, professors would add, remove, or alter assignments in the middle of the semester that we would also have to remember to change in our personal calendars. There had to be an easier way to simplify this process. And that's when AssignHub manifested.

Through AssignHub we hope to make college students lives easier by providing a platform where they can view all of their assignments with ease grouped by the classes they are taking. The crowdsourcing feature allows students to add assignments for each of their classes without having to manually input them in which will truly save students so much time. At the end of the day, our focus has always been on improving our peer's mental health. The remote environment has stripped many student's of their social lives and we wanted to make sure our peers continue to have a place where they can check up on their friend's mental health. With a click of a button, every student can share how they are feeling through an emoji and they can see their friend's emotions too.

We hope that our solution will save you time that you can use on yourself. No matter how busy you are, always remember that your mental health should always take priority.
