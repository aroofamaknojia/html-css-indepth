More Than Just a Calendar

1

Crowdsource Your Assignments

+

Make it easier on everyone in your class and crowdsource your assignment while manually adding it in your schedule. You'll be helping your peers save time and we'll make sure fake assignments and events are removed from the system through our two submission approval process.

2

Check up on Your Friend's Mental Health

+

Be able to see how your friends are feeling through the daily emoji checker. You can add your friends to your dashboard and even display your own emoji for the day. While being seperated from your college friends, it is more important than ever that you check up on each other's mental health.

3

Form Study Groups

+

You can find peers in your class to study with when you crowdsource the session, so everyone in your class that benefits from peer review is able to come together on one zoom call. In a virtual format, its harder to form study sessions like you used to with peers that were sitting next to you in class. We make it easier than ever by bringing together individuals to get the job done.

4

View All of Your Due Dates

+

The calendar on your dashboard allows you to see your assignments for all of your classes through the weekly view and the monthly view. You can cross your assignments off the todo list located right next your calendar and your finished assignments will automatically move to the completed assignment section. Organize your life better during the pandemic through AssignHub!
